Section 1
Services Provided
Curbloom agrees to provide the following each month, based on the selected plan above:
  • A 30-day content calendar mapping post type, platform, and topic for each day of the month
  • 30 fully written social media captions, voice-matched to the client
  • Designed graphics for all 30 posts (Growth and Premium only)
  • Full scheduling on the client's behalf (Premium only)
  • Monthly performance report PDF (Premium only)
Content is delivered by the 1st of each month for the month ahead. One round of revisions is included per month.
Section 2
Exclusive Territory
🔒 Territory Lock

By signing this agreement, the client's community and market area is exclusively reserved. Curbloom will not provide services to any competing agent operating in the same community or zip code area for the duration of this agreement. This exclusivity is the client's upon signing and is maintained as long as the account remains active.

Section 3
Payment Terms
The monthly retainer fee is due on the 1st of each month. Clients who sign during the founding period lock in their rate permanently — pricing will not increase as long as the account remains active and in good standing.

Accepted payment methods will be confirmed at onboarding. A 7-day grace period applies before service is paused for non-payment.
Section 4
Term & Cancellation
This agreement is month-to-month with no long-term commitment required. Either party may cancel with 30 days written notice. Upon cancellation, the client retains all content delivered up to that point. The exclusive territory lock is released upon cancellation, making the market available to other agents.
Section 5
Client Responsibilities
The client agrees to:
  • Complete the onboarding questionnaire before service begins
  • Provide any personal photos or market-specific information requested for content
  • Post content according to the calendar provided (Founder and Growth tiers)
  • Provide platform access when required (Premium tier)
  • Review and approve content within 5 business days of delivery
Curbloom is not responsible for the performance of posts once published. Results depend on consistency, audience, and posting practices.
Section 6
Ownership & Confidentiality
All content produced by Curbloom for the client is the client's property upon delivery. Curbloom retains the right to reference the engagement for portfolio or case study purposes unless the client requests otherwise in writing.

Both parties agree to keep business terms and proprietary methods confidential.
